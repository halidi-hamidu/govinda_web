About Us

Govinda Digital Print Ltd is a company registered with the government of Tanzania and granted full certification (B.L 20000036853).

Empowering Imagination, One Print at a Time.

Govinda Digital Printing Company is a creative haven where ideas take shape on paper. With a passion for precision and an eye for innovation, we transform concepts into captivating prints. Your vision is our canvas, and together, we craft stories that leave a lasting impression.

Our Vision: we strive to redefine the art of printing, combining technology and creativity to deliver unparalleled quality and inspiration with every design that graces our paper.

Location: Nyamwezi/Rufiji Street Kariakoo, Dar es salaam
